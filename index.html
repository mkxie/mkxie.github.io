Experience

High Perfomance Computing Intern

Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory

This past summer, I got the amazing opportunity to work for Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory in Livermore, CA, home of the third fastest supercomputer in the world. As part of my internship, I installed and configured my own high performance computing clusters, and then I learned how to automate the entire process using Ansible. Additionally, I worked on expanding a suite of Python scripts using the open source Pyvmomi API. Our software, Livmomi, is used throughout Livermore Computing and the Systems Administration Group at the lab in order to manage and gather information about virtual machines. What used to take minutes using VMware's ESXi GUI now takes seconds with Livmomi.
